Student Loans

Led by the Financial Aid Office

Study Abroad

Led by the Global Education Center

  • Recommended for anyone planning to go abroad in the next year
  • Officially one step towards receiving the Study Abroad ELR credit
  • Affects the 80+% of Elon's population that goes abroad.

Food and Diet

Led by the Kernodle Center

  • Recommended to all students who will be going off of the Elon meal plan for the first time
  • Covers how to meal prep and how to budget for nutritious meals
  • Student discounts offered at our partner institutions, restaurants, and grocery stores

Insurance

Led by the Financial Education Center

  • Recommended to any students who are living off campus
  • Recommended to any student leasing or purchasing a car
  • Recommended for any student graduating and becoming a renter for the first time

Retirement

Led by the Student Professional Development Center

  • Retirement planning is most advantageous when started at a young age

Investments
